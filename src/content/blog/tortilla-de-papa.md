A classic Spanish tortilla made with tender potatoes, eggs, and onions, cooked slowly for a rich, comforting flavor.

- **Prep** 15min
- **Cook** 35min
- **Total** 50min
- **Yield** 4 servings

## Ingredients

- 5 medium potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
- 1 large onion, thinly sliced
- 6 large eggs
- 120ml olive oil
- Salt to taste

## Instructions

- Heat olive oil in a large non-stick pan over medium heat.
- Add the sliced potatoes and onion, season with salt, and cook gently for 20–25 minutes until soft but not browned.
- Drain the potatoes and onion, reserving a little oil.
- In a large bowl, beat the eggs and add the cooked potato mixture. Mix well.
- Heat a small amount of the reserved oil in the pan over medium heat.
- Pour in the mixture and cook for 5–7 minutes until the bottom is set.
- Carefully flip the tortilla using a plate and cook the other side for another 5 minutes.
- Let it rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
