A soft and aromatic orange cake popular in Colombia and Venezuela, made with fresh orange juice and zest for a bright citrus flavor.

- **Prep** 20min
- **Cook** 45min
- **Total** 1h 5min
- **Yield** 8 servings

## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter (softened) or vegetable oil
- 3 eggs
- 1 cup fresh orange juice
- 1 tablespoon orange zest
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt

## Instructions

- Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F) and grease a cake pan.
- In a bowl, cream the butter (or oil) with the sugar until light and smooth.
- Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
- Stir in the orange juice, orange zest, and vanilla extract.
- In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
- Gradually incorporate the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until fully combined.
- Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top.
- Bake for 40–45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
- Let the cake cool before removing from the pan and serving.
