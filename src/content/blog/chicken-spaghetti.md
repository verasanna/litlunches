A flavorful chicken spaghetti dish with Caribbean spices, vegetables, and a light tomato-based sauce.

- **Prep** 15min
- **Cook** 35min
- **Total** 50min
- **Yield** 4 servings

## Ingredients

- 300g spaghetti
- 2 chicken breasts, sliced into strips
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 200g crushed tomatoes
- 100ml chicken broth
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1/2 tsp dried oregano
- 1/2 tsp ground cumin
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- Fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish
- Grated cheese (optional)

## Instructions

- Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
- Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chicken strips, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned and cooked through. Remove and set aside.
- In the same pan, sauté the onion, garlic, and red bell pepper until softened.
- Add crushed tomatoes, chicken broth, paprika, oregano, and cumin. Stir well and let simmer for 10 minutes.
- Return the cooked chicken to the pan and mix with the sauce.
- Add the cooked spaghetti and toss until evenly coated with the sauce.
- Adjust seasoning if needed. Serve hot, garnished with fresh herbs and optional grated cheese.
