A simple Caribbean-style fried cheese dish with a crisp golden crust and a soft, warm center, typically served as an appetizer or snack.

- **Prep** 10min
- **Cook** 10min
- **Total** 20min
- **Yield** 4 servings

## Ingredients

- 400g firm white cheese (queso blanco or frying cheese)
- 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (optional, for light coating)
- Vegetable oil for frying
- Salt to taste
- Lime wedges (optional, for serving)
- Honey or guava sauce (optional, for serving)

## Instructions

- Cut the cheese into thick slices or rectangular pieces.
- Pat the cheese dry with paper towels to reduce excess moisture.
- Lightly coat the cheese pieces in flour if using, shaking off excess.
- Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
- Fry the cheese pieces until golden brown on each side, about 1–2 minutes per side.
- Remove and drain on paper towels.
- Sprinkle lightly with salt and serve immediately with lime wedges or sweet sauce if desired.
