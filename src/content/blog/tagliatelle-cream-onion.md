A simple and comforting pasta dish featuring tender tagliatelle coated in a rich cream and caramelized onion sauce.

- **Prep** 10min
- **Cook** 25min
- **Total** 35min
- **Yield** 4 servings

## Ingredients

- 400g tagliatelle (or similar pasta)
- 2 large onions, thinly sliced
- 250ml heavy cream
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 50g grated Parmesan cheese
- Salt and black pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

## Instructions

- Cook the tagliatelle in a large pot of salted boiling water according to package instructions. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain.
- In a large pan, heat olive oil and butter over medium heat.
- Add the sliced onions and cook slowly for 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until soft and lightly caramelized.
- Add the minced garlic and cook for 1–2 minutes until fragrant.
- Pour in the heavy cream and stir well. Let it simmer gently for 5 minutes until slightly thickened.
- Add the cooked pasta to the sauce, tossing to coat evenly. Use reserved pasta water if needed to loosen the sauce.
- Stir in grated Parmesan cheese and season with salt and black pepper.
- Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley if desired.
