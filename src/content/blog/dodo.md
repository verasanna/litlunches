A simple West African dish of ripe plantains fried until golden and caramelised. Commonly served as a side or snack.

- **Prep** 10min
- **Cook** 15min
- **Total** 25min
- **Yield** 4 servings

## Ingredients

- 3 ripe plantains (yellow with black spots)
- Vegetable oil for frying
- Salt to taste
- Optional: pinch of chili powder
- Optional: 1 teaspoon sugar

## Instructions

- Peel the plantains and slice them diagonally into medium-thick pieces.
- Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
- Fry the plantain slices in batches until golden brown on both sides.
- Remove and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
- Sprinkle lightly with salt (and optional chili or sugar) and serve warm.
