Crispy golden fried yam slices, a popular West African snack or side dish, typically served with spicy pepper sauce or eggs.

- **Prep** 15min
- **Cook** 20min
- **Total** 35min
- **Yield** 4 servings

## Ingredients

- 1 medium yam tuber
- Vegetable oil for deep frying
- Salt to taste
- Freshly ground black pepper (optional)
- Cayenne pepper or chili flakes (optional)

## Instructions

- Peel the yam and cut it into thick or medium-sized slices or sticks.
- Rinse the yam pieces in cold water to remove excess starch, then pat dry thoroughly.
- Season lightly with salt and optional spices.
- Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan to medium-high temperature.
- Fry the yam pieces in batches until golden brown and crisp on the outside, about 5–7 minutes per batch.
- Remove and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
- Serve hot, optionally with spicy pepper sauce, eggs, or a dipping sauce of choice.
