A fresh and vibrant salad combining creamy avocado, crisp green beans, and sweet pineapple with a light citrus dressing.

- **Prep** 15min
- **Cook** 5min
- **Total** 20min
- **Yield** 4 servings

## Ingredients

- 2 ripe avocados, diced
- 200g green beans (habichuelas), trimmed
- 1 cup fresh pineapple, diced
- 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh cilantro or parsley (optional)

## Instructions

- Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and blanch the green beans for 3–5 minutes until tender-crisp. Drain and cool under cold water.
- In a large bowl, combine the green beans, diced avocado, pineapple, and red onion.
- In a small bowl, whisk together lime juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
- Pour the dressing over the salad and gently toss to combine.
- Garnish with fresh cilantro or parsley if desired.
- Serve immediately or chill briefly before serving.
